HEY! WELCOME TO hazelnut factory—

OUR STORY

The Hazelnut Factory in Lucknow is a beloved café and dessert destination that began with a simple passion for creating high-quality, handcrafted confections. Founded in 2014 by Aman Singh, the café started as a boutique bakery offering artisanal chocolates, pastries, and desserts. With its charming European-inspired decor and cozy ambiance, it quickly became a local favorite, attracting dessert lovers from all over the city.

What set The Hazelnut Factory apart was its dedication to using premium ingredients and crafting every item with precision and creativity. Signature items like the hazelnut praline cake and Belgian chocolate truffles earned it a loyal customer base. Over time, the café expanded its menu to include gourmet sandwiches, pizzas, and coffees, evolving into a full-service café while maintaining its dessert legacy.

Located in the upscale area of Hazratganj, the café became more than just a food destination—it turned into a social hub for young professionals, families, and students. The brand's success led to multiple outlets in Lucknow, each maintaining the same quality and warmth. Today, The Hazelnut Factory stands as a symbol of modern Lucknow's evolving food scene, blending global tastes with local love and passion for excellence.

— Ankit Sahni, Founder, Hazelnut Factory

GREAT CAFE EXPERIENCE

YOUR HOST

Ankit Sahni is the founder and owner of the hazelnut factory. She started her career with selling stained glass paintings in exhibitions all over India.

Ankit Sahni is the founder and CEO of The Hazelnut Factory (THF), a prominent Lucknow-based café and artisanal sweets brand. Driven by a passion to create a café that seamlessly blends coffee, bakery products, and sweets, Sahni embarked on this journey in 2018. After dedicating a year to meticulously crafting the concept, he launched the first THF store in 2019.

Under Sahni's leadership, THF has experienced significant growth, expanding to over 18 outlets across cities like Lucknow, Kanpur, and Delhi. The brand offers a diverse menu featuring vegetarian dishes, egg-based bakery items, and a variety of gluten-free and vegan options. Sahni emphasizes excellence in every product, ensuring a unique experience for customers.

In a strategic move to scale operations, THF secured a ₹131 crore investment from Bikaji Foods International in 2024, with Bikaji acquiring a 53.02% stake in the company. This partnership aims to propel THF's expansion into quick commerce and establish a presence in transit retail locations like airports and metro stations. Sahni envisions opening a new outlet every month over the next three years, targeting cities across Uttar Pradesh and the National Capital Region.

Sahni's entrepreneurial journey with THF reflects a commitment to blending traditional Indian sweets with modern café culture, positioning the brand as a significant player in India's evolving food and beverage industry.
