ABOUT
Roastry Coffee House is a cozy and inviting café dedicated to serving freshly roasted, premium-quality coffee. With a passion for craftsmanship, we source the finest beans from around the world and roast them in-house to ensure bold flavor and rich aroma in every cup. Whether you're looking for a quiet place to work, catch up with friends, or simply enjoy a delicious brew, Roastry offers the perfect atmosphere and exceptional service for every coffee lover.
FOUNDER
Roastery Coffee House in Lucknow was founded by Nishant Sinha, a hospitality graduate from IHM Jodhpur. With experience at Café Coffee Day, Lavazza, and HUL, he launched the first Roastery in Hyderabad in 2017. The Lucknow outlet, opened in 2022, reflects his vision of bringing specialty Indian coffee to diverse cities. Sinha emphasizes sustainable sourcing, paying premium prices to farmers, and creating inviting spaces that blend heritage with modern café culture.
