ABOUT
The Terrace, nestled in the heart of Aliganj, Lucknow, is a charming rooftop café celebrated for its cozy ambiance and tasteful décor. Renowned for its artisanal beverages and fusion delicacies, The Terrace offers guests a tranquil escape with scenic rooftop views, ambient lighting, and live music sessions. Whether it’s a brunch with friends or a romantic evening, The Terrace provides a perfect blend of comfort, flavor, and elegance, making it a top choice for urban diners seeking both vibe and taste.
FOUNDER
The "Terrace" in Hazratganj refers to the Lawrence Terrace, which is one of the historical kothis (buildings) in the area. It was not founded by a single person, but rather emerged as part of the development of Hazratganj, which was initially identified by Nawab Saadat Ali Khan in 1810. He began creating European-style kothis in the area, including the one that would later be known as Lawrence Terrace.
