The Terrace Café

The Terrace Café in Lucknow, located on the 4th floor of Hotel Charans Plaza in Hazratganj, is a popular casual dining spot known for its vibrant ambiance and diverse menu. Offering both indoor and outdoor seating, it's an ideal place for gatherings, parties, or a relaxing meal. The café serves a wide range of cuisines including North Indian, fast food, pizzas, and Indo-Chinese dishes, with popular options like Paneer Butter Masala, Dal Makhani, and Manchurian combos. Open daily from 12 PM to midnight, The Terrace has received mixed reviews—many praise the food and setting, while some mention service-related issues. It also offers parking facilities, serves alcohol, and can accommodate up to 60 guests for events. Whether you're in the mood for a cozy meal or a lively dinner with friends, The Terrace is a notable option in the heart of Lucknow.
